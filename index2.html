Welcome to Kulob Team Records

Kulob Team Records is a music label founded in 2015 with the goal of supporting and promoting talented artists from around the world. We believe that every artist deserves the opportunity to be heard, and our mission is to provide a platform for their creativity and help them shine on the global stage.

Our Values:

  • Support for unique and creative projects.
  • Creating high-quality and relevant music.
  • Promoting artists on the international stage.

Our Mission

We strive to be the support for every artist, helping them unlock their potential and win the hearts of listeners worldwide. We not only create music, but also develop your image, promote you on major platforms, and create collaborations with leading music brands. Your goal is to create, our goal is to help you be heard!

Our Playlists

We are proud to present our playlists, which include unique compositions from our artists. They can be heard on major music platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, and others. We create the atmosphere for your sound, providing the opportunity to be featured in exclusive playlists and expand your audience. Be inspired and create new musical masterpieces with us!
